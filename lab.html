Notable Mars Missions

Curiosity Rover

The Curiosity rover has been exploring Mars since 2012. It has provided invaluable data about the planet's geology and atmosphere.

Perseverance Rover

Perseverance landed on Mars in February 2021 and is studying the planet's geology and searching for signs of ancient life.

Mission Objectives

  • Analyze Mars' geology
  • Search for signs of past life
  • Collect soil and rock samples
